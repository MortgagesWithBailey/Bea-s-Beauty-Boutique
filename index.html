Meet Brea

Hi, I'm Brea, the owner and artist behind Brea's Beauty Boutique. I started my business in 2024 with a passion for helping people feel confident and beautiful.

My journey began with my own nails. They were naturally weak and prone to breaking so I discovered the benefits of gel nails. Over time, I realized how much having beautiful nails and later, lashes boosted my confidence and made me feel my best. That feeling inspired me to create the same experience for others.

Today, I specialize in a wide range of services in a welcoming, relaxing environment where every client feels valued. Whether you're getting ready for a special occasion or simply pampering yourself, my goal is for you to leave feeling refreshed, confident, and excited to show off your new look.

I can't wait to welcome you and help you feel as amazing as you are.
